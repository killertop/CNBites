Carrot Knife-Cut Steamed Buns Recipe

Ingredients

  • Self-raising flour for steamed buns: 220g
  • Fresh carrots: 220g
  • Drinking water: 80g
  • Powdered sugar: 25g

Instructions

  1. Peel the carrots, cut into chunks, add water, and blend into carrot puree without filtering.
  2. Prepare the self-raising flour for steamed buns.
  3. Mix the self-raising flour with carrot puree and sugar until combined.
  4. Knead into a smooth dough.
  5. Roll out the dough and release air bubbles.
  6. Roll up and cut into pieces.
  7. Cut into evenly sized pieces.
  8. Place in a steaming basket, let ferment until doubled in size, and steam over medium heat for 15 minutes.
  9. Delicious and healthy carrot steamed buns are ready!
