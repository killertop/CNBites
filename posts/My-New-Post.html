Ingredients

  • Half a pound of pork ribs (about 250 grams)
  • Green onion strips, as needed
  • Ginger strips, as needed
  • Shaoxing wine, a small amount
  • Pepper powder, a small amount
  • Minced garlic, one head
  • Chili pepper, one

Instructions

  1. Cut the ribs into 4-5 cm segments, add Shaoxing wine, ginger strips, green onion strips, salt, pepper powder, and Sichuan pepper powder to marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic and chili pepper segments for garnish, set aside on an empty plate.
  3. The marinated ribs should look nicely colored.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, bring the oil to medium heat, and fry the marinated ribs.
  5. After frying, place the ribs on a dish.
  6. Add a little oil to the pot, fry the minced garlic until fragrant, add the ribs and chili segments, stir fry and then remove from heat.

Cooking Tips

  • Adjust the frying time according to personal taste. For those who prefer tender meat, control the frying time and heat.
